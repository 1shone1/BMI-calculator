What is BMI?

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a measure that uses height and weight to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height.

Importance of BMI

It helps assess health risks associated with being overweight or underweight.

Risks of Being Overweight or Underweight

Underweight Risks:

  • Malnutrition
  • Weakened immune system
  • Osteoporosis
  • Fertility issues
