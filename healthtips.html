Health & Wellness Tips

Your guide to a healthier lifestyle

Nutrition Basics

Remember: A balanced diet is key to maintaining a healthy BMI.

  • Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains
  • Include lean proteins in your diet
  • Limit processed foods and added sugars
  • Stay hydrated with water

Exercise Guidelines

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate exercise weekly.

  • Start with 30 minutes of walking daily
  • Include strength training 2-3 times per week
  • Try different activities to stay motivated
  • Listen to your body and rest when needed
  • Make exercise a social activity

Sleep & Recovery

Quality sleep is essential for overall health and weight management.

  • Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep nightly
  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • Limit screen time before bed
  • Keep your bedroom cool and dark

Stress Management

Managing stress is crucial for maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Try meditation or mindfulness
  • Stay connected with friends and family
  • Take regular breaks during work
  • Engage in hobbies you enjoy
