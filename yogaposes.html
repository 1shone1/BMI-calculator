Yoga Poses for a Healthier You

Improve flexibility, balance, and peace of mind through yoga.

Popular Yoga Poses

Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

Foundation for all standing poses. Improves posture, grounding, and stability.

Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Strengthens arms and legs, stretches the spine, and relieves stress.

Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)

Increases stamina and strengthens legs, opens chest and shoulders.

Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

Enhances balance, stability, and strengthens core muscles.
