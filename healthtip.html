Health Tips for a Better BMI

Understand your BMI and improve your health!

Health Tips

Stay Active

Exercise regularly, even light walking 30 minutes a day can make a big difference!

Eat Balanced Meals

Include fruits, vegetables, proteins, and whole grains in your meals daily.

Stay Hydrated

Drinking water helps control appetite and supports body functions.

Get Enough Sleep

Proper sleep helps maintain metabolism and lowers stress.

Track Progress

Regularly monitor your BMI and weight to stay on the right track.

How BMI Works

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a number calculated using your weight and height: BMI = weight (kg) / height² (m²). It helps categorize your body status:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: BMI ≥ 30
